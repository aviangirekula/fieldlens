New HVAC techs are thrown onto live equipment fast, with little supervision. Mistakes are expensive, callbacks hurt, and some are dangerous.

Safety

Safety is the product, not a feature.

FieldLens is built for someone nervous, standing next to lethal voltage. When it isn't certain, it chooses the more cautious verdict. It never tells a new tech to touch, loosen, or probe anything without confirmed lockout/tagout and zero-voltage verification.

Safe to inspect Caution required Do not touch

AI-generated guidance. Always verify with a qualified supervisor. FieldLens is a training aid, not a substitute for proper training, procedures, or PPE.

Defensibility
